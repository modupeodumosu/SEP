How it works

A directory built on trust, not just sign-ups.

Entry is by invitation from SEP attendance or community vouching — so every member is genuinely connected to the OneChurch family.

01 · Attend or be vouched

Earn your place

Attend SEP 2026 (or be vouched by an existing member) to qualify for an invitation. This keeps the directory genuine and useful — not another list of strangers.

02 · Complete your profile

Show your work

Add your skills, business, and the kinds of connections you're open to — referrals, mentorship, collaboration, or just community. Your profile is only visible to other members.

03 · Connect and grow

Find your people

Browse by skill, industry, or location. Reach out directly. No algorithm, no ads — just a trusted room of people you already share values with.
