The SEP Hub Directory.

A trusted directory of business owners, entrepreneurs, and skilled professionals from our church community.

What it is

Find, connect, and collaborate - inside a community that already trusts each other.

The SEP Hub Directory is a searchable directory of people from the SEP and OneChurch community - business owners, freelancers, skilled professionals, and service providers. You can browse by skill or business category, find the right person, and reach out directly. No cold searches. No strangers.

It's not a job board or a marketplace. It's more like a trusted room - built on the understanding that everyone in it is already part of the same community. When you hire from the directory, you're hiring someone your neighbour can already vouch for.

How trust works

Not just a sign-up list.
A verified community.

Anyone can build a directory. What makes the Hub different is what we check before a profile goes live.

01 · Identity

Verified identity

Before your profile goes live, we confirm you are who you say you are. Every listing is a real person with a real name. No anonymous entries, no fake profiles.

02 · Community

Church community membership

Every member is connected to the OneChurch or SEP community. That shared foundation is the trust layer. You're not browsing strangers - you're finding people who already share your values.

03 · Profile

Real profiles, real work

Profiles show what you actually do - your skills, your business, what you're open to. No inflated titles or vague bios. What you see is honest and useful.

Who it's for

Built for everyone in the community.

Whether you're building something, offering something, or looking for the right person - the Hub is for you.

Business owners

Get found by people who already trust you

List your business and reach community members who want to support local and trusted. Your next customer may already be in the same room every Sunday.

Freelancers & creatives

Put your skills in front of a warm audience

Showcase what you do to a community that already values you as a person - not just a service provider. Warm leads from the start.

Professionals

Connect, mentor, and be mentored

Find others in your field, offer what you know, or find someone whose experience you can learn from. Iron sharpens iron.

Hirers & referrers

Search and engage with confidence

Need a photographer, developer, caterer, or financial consultant? Browse by category and engage knowing the person has already been vetted by the community.
