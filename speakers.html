Keynote
Building a business that outlasts you

What happens to your business when you're no longer in the room? This keynote goes beyond profit to the deeper work of building something that endures - a business anchored in values, run with integrity, and structured to outlive its founder. We'll explore how your convictions shape your culture, your decisions, and your legacy, and why the businesses that last are built on more than ambition.

Building a global brand from scratch

How do you take a business born in Lagos to the world? This session unpacks what it really takes to build a brand that travels - positioning, storytelling, quality standards, and the mindset shift from local hustle to global player. Practical lessons from someone who has done it, for anyone ready to think bigger than their current market.

The creator economy

The private life behind the public work. Building an online platform looks exciting from the outside - but behind every post is a real person managing pressure, comparison, and the question of who they are when the camera is off. An honest panel on building and monetising a platform without losing yourself: staying rooted in faith, handling visibility, and creating with substance.
