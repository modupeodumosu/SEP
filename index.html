What SEP is

A 3-day training built for people who want to do the work, not just hear about it.

SEP is the flagship event of OneChurch International's Kingdom Impact Team. Whether you're starting a business, switching careers, or looking to sharpen a skill you can charge for — SEP is designed to send you home with something you can use.
